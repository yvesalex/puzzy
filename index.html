Puzzy Squares

The culture of patience.

Puzzy Squares is a free puzzle game. You just need to click on, or slide the squares to form the beautiful picture.

Play & Shots


Chose a picture, set the game level, start to play.
Anytime you need to preview the target, click on eye icon button on top right side.
Move the squares until completion. You are a winner!
